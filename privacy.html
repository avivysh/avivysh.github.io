Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to the portfolio of Aviv ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and interact with our AI Marketing Advisory services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Data

When you use our contact form or subscribe to updates, we may collect your name, email address, company name, and any information you provide in your message.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our site, including IP addresses, browser types, and pages visited.

AI Interaction Data

Messages sent to our "AI Advisor" tool are processed to provide real-time responses. Please do not share sensitive personal or proprietary business information in the AI chat.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our AI Marketing Advisory services.
  • To respond to your inquiries and consultation requests.
  • To analyze website performance and user engagement.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraudulent activities.

4. AI Data Processing & Third Parties

Our AI Advisor utilizes the Google Gemini API for natural language processing. When you interact with the AI, your input is sent to Google's servers. We do not store these conversations on our local servers for longer than the current session duration unless required for service improvement. Google's data handling is governed by their own privacy policies.

5. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate information.
  • Erasure: Request that we delete your personal data.
  • Portability: Request a transfer of your data to another service.
  • Opt-out: Object to certain types of data processing.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to ensure the website functions correctly. We may also use analytical cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
